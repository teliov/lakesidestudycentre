LAKESIDE

What is Lakeside Study Centre

Lakeside Study Centre is a project of Educational Corporate Society (ECS), a non-profit organization.

It offers young men human, social and professional training: giving them the tools that they need to be not only academically excellent and responsible citizens, but also God fearing individuals and future professionals.

Lakeside is dedicated to seeing a new generation of young men with integrity, high moral standards and positive systems. The plethora of formation provided at Lakeside encompasses the human, professional, spiritual, cultural and social facets of the individual’s personality.

This is aimed at making each person a well-rounded and fully developed gentleman.

The spiritual formation in Lakeside Study Centre is entrusted to Opus Dei, a prelature of the Catholic Church.

What is Opus Dei?

Opus Dei is a Catholic institution founded by Saint Josemaría Escrivá. Its mission is to help people turn their work and daily activities into occasions for growing closer to God, for serving others, and for improving society.

Opus Dei complements the work of local churches by offering classes, talks, retreats and pastoral care that help people develop their personal spiritual life and apostolate. As the Second Vatican Council noted, every baptized person is called to follow Jesus Christ, by living according to the Gospel and making it known to others.

The aim of Opus Dei is to contribute to that evangelizing mission of the Church, by promoting among Christians of all social classes a life fully consistent with their faith, in the middle of the ordinary circumstances of their lives and especially through the sanctification of their work.

Opus Dei’s main activity consists in offering its members, and other people, the spiritual means they need to live as good Christians in the midst of the world.

For more information on Opus Dei, visit the Opus Dei website
