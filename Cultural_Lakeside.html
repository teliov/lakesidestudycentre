Activities

AVAILABLE ACTIVITIES

The activities offered at Lakeside Study Centre cover the human, social, professional, academic and spiritual training needed to improve and live a fully fulfilled and meaningful life.

Cultural activities

With the aim of helping the participants to be refined gentlemen, various activities are organized to improve their cultural training.

This includes workshops, discussion forums, presentations on notable fields of knowledge, literature, personalities, etc. The participants, not only listen, but also contribute their own share of the knowledge to be shared in such activities

Academics

There is a state-of-the-art library open from 10am to 9pm on weekdays and 9am to 9pm on weekends.

In addition, students are invited to learn how to improve their skills, both theoretical and practical. The activities range from seminars on study skills and time management, to courses in different fields of learning eg. Engineering, Information and Communications Technology, etc.

They also acquire life skills that will be useful for them in their academic life, and also the professional world.

Get-togethers

Talented individuals are regularly invited to interact with the students and young professionals in an informal setting. These gatherings, commonly called get-togethers, are aimed at improving the knowledge and experience of all in attendance.

The topics range from the industrial technology industry, the experiences of an enterpreneur, life lessons of successful people, to history of different places, music, art, politics and any other topic under the sun. Everybody is invited to ask questions and also contribute one's insight, thereby learning while sharing what one has.

Spiritual activities

Opportunities are made available to improve the moral and ethical experience of the people in contact with the centre. This is done through interactive forums for discussing current moral issues, monthly days of recollection and also yearly retreats.

This is because neglecting the soul while improving the mind and body is, in the end, utter folly. A priest of the Catholic Church is regularly available for personal spiritual direction on Tuesday and Wednesdays throughout the day, and at varied times of the day during the rest of the week. Sports and Excursions

As is well-known, rest is a vital part of the life of any person. To provide this, opportunities for sports and excursions are made.

Weekly sports activities are organized from Lakeside Study Centre, principally football and badminton.

Excursions are also organized to interesting and engaging places, to help people enjoy themselves while expanding their horizons.
